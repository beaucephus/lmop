====== Undil MountainRiver ======
Created Wednesday 24 April 2019

Adult mail mountain dwarf dressed in merchants travel clothes.  He travels by a large merchant's wagon pulled by 2 large mountain goats.
Very long and straight brown hair and well-oiled also straight, brown beard.

**Mannerisms**
He is a fast-talker and is not afraid to pull a con.
He has a bad habit of interrupting people.

**Motivation**
Undil is a travelling merchant.  From his wagon he trades in magical items and oddities.
He wants to see the world and uses trade as his means to do so.
If the players mention [[Gundren Rockseeker]]
  Undil wants to gain favor within the dwarf clans will give the players a discount in exchange for a meeting with [[Gundren Rockseeker]]

**Loot/Trade**
5400GP
Cloak of Protection - 2000GP
3 Potions of Greater Healing - 250GP each
5 Potions of Healing - 50GP each
