====== Undil MountainRiver ======
Created Wednesday 24 April 2019

Adult mail mountain dwarf merchant.
Very long and straight brown hair and well-oiled beard.

**Mannerisms**
He is a fast-talker and is not afraid to pull a con.
He has a bad habit of interrupting people.

**Motivation**
Undil is a travelling merchant.  From his wagon he trades in magical items and oddities.
He wants to see the world and uses trade as his means to do so.

**Loot**
5400GP
Cloak of Protection
3 Potions of Greater Healing
5 Potions of Healing
Merchant's Wagon
