====== Cragmaw Goblins ======
Created Sunday 13 January 2019

The Cragmaw are new rag-tag tribe of goblins, hobgoblins, and bugbears.
They have been organized under [[King Grol of Cragmaw Castle]] who is loyal to [[Nezznar the Black Spider]] after Nezznar helped him unite the tribes.
