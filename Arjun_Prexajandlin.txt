====== Arjun Prexajandlin ======
Created Sunday 17 February 2019

Arjun is a male dragonborn life cleric.
His Deity is [[Bahamut the Platinum Dragon]]

Arjun owes his life to the priest who took him in when his parents died
Arjun is gullible when interacting with anyone who profess faith in Bahamut
