====== Black Ethel ======
Created Wednesday 17 July 2019

A **night hag** in the [[:Beggar's Nest Coven]] of [[:Neverwinter]]

Black Ethel "prepares" the children of the [[:Little Miracles Orphan Home]] for consumption.
This involves giving them nightmares and corrupting them into depraved, evil souls.

run [[:Dungeon:Beggar's Nest Coven Lair]]
