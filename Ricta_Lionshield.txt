====== Ricta Lionshield ======
Created Sunday 14 April 2019

Ricta is a lesser ranking noble of the Lionshield family.

**Motivation**
Ricta wants to sell the [[Lionshield Coster]] and return to [[Neverwinter]] ASAP.

**Looks and Personality**
Ricta is a 25-year-old human female of dark complexion and friendly manner.
She has black hair pulled up into twin poytails.
She fancies [[Othard Drapier]] and will flirt with him if given the chance.

**Loot**
122GP
