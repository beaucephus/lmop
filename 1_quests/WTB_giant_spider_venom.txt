====== WTB giant spider venom ======
Created Friday 08 February 2019

[[Theodore Anthony Nugent]] has posted a quest to the [[Phandalin]] town board to buy giant spider venom

//Want to buy: Giant Spider Venom.  40gp per liter. Come inquire at the Phandalin medical office on the eastern side of town.  ~Theodore Anthony Nugent//

He uses the venom to make various potions including Potions of Healing.

He also knows that there are many giant spiders near the [[Ruins of Thundertree]] and will provide 5 empty vials to anybody who agrees to go to Thundertree to harvest the spider venom.
