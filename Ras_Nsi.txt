====== Ras Nsi ======
Created Thursday 14 March 2019

Ras Nsi is the leader of the yuan-ti faction on the island of [[Chult]]

Ras Nsi sent [[Atan]] on a mission to find the [[Forge of Spells]] and take control of it for the yuan-ti.
