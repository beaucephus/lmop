====== Sanctified Bones ======
Created Sunday 02 June 2019

Plain wooden tinder box.  Contains a human mandible + some carpals (DC10 check to discern the race and type of bones).

The bones emit an obvious magical aura.

The bones rattle about inside the box at an audible lie.
