====== Ring of Shielding ======
Created Friday 12 July 2019


Ring, Uncommon (requires attunement)

//The band is a thick loop of silver with a heavy dome-cut sapphire set in the socket. The ring feels heavier than its size suggests it should.//

This ring has 3 charges. While wearing it, you can expend 1 charge as an reaction to cast the Shield spell from it. The ring regains 1d3 expended charges daily at dawn.
