====== Potion of Heroism ======
Created Thursday 30 May 2019

This blue potion bubbles and steams as if boiling.

Upon drinking you gain 10 Temporary Hit Points that last for 1 hour. For the same Duration, you are under the effect of the bless spell (no Concentration required).
