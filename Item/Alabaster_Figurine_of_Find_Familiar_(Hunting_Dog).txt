====== Alabaster Figurine of Find Familiar (Hunting Dog) ======
Created Thursday 30 May 2019

Small, finely crafted alabaster figurine in the shape of a hunting dog.

Command word: Alabaster (It's the dog's name. An identify spell reveals the name).

Hold the figurine in hand and speak the command word to summon a familiar a la the find familiar spell.
The familiar takes the shape of a hunting dog and only obeys commands from the holder of the figurine.
