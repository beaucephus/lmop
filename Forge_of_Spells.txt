====== Forge of Spells ======
Created Sunday 13 January 2019

The Forge of Spells is the magical forge made legendary by tails of the [[Lost Mine of Phandelver]]
It is located in [[Wave Echo Cave]]
When properly manned and worked (requires a large crew), it can create magical items.
