====== Vyerith ======
Created Sunday 13 January 2019

Vyerith is a female drow spy.

**Motivation**
Vyerith is a loyal lieutenant of [[Lythrana Yril'Lysaen]]
She wants to complete her mission which is to either retrieve or destroy all information regarding [[Wave Echo Cave]]

**Personality**
She is a smooth talker and never raises her voice.

**Knowledge**
Vyerith is very tight-lipped and trained to resist torture and interrogation.
Vyerith knows that Nezznar is in [[Wave Echo Cave]]
She knows why the drow are here on the surface (To utilize the [[Forge of Spells]] to craft magical weapons in order to turn the tide in their war with the deurgar in the underdark.)
