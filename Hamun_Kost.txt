====== Hamun Kost ======
Created Friday 11 January 2019

Hamun Kost is a high level necromancer who has set up tent outside the [[Old owl well]].  He is there searching for the banshee [[Agatha]] and her spell book.
He keeps 12 skeletons in a tower near the well, and lets them out occasionally to scavenge, hunt, gather for him.
