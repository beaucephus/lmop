====== King Grol of Cragmaw Castle ======
Created Sunday 13 January 2019

King Grol is an old bugbear chief who has united the local goblinoid populations under his banner with the help of [[Nezznar the Black Spider]]
he rules the [[Cragmaw Goblins]] out of [[Cragmaw Castle]] with his pet wolf, Snarl
