====== Walddi Wentonc ======
Created Tuesday 05 February 2019

Walddi is a young (18) recent recruit of the [[Redbrands]]
He comes from the Wentoc family, a broken family.

**Motivation**
He wants to escape his abusive father and mother.

**Personality**
He is usure and afraid.  He doesn't look the party in the eye.

**Knowledge**
Walddi is faily new to the Redbrands and doesn't know much.
The Redbrands are led by a human man that can cast spells.  They call him Glasstaff ([[Iarno Albrek aka Glasstaff]])
Their hideout in town is the [[Sleeping Giant]] , but their leader is at the [[Redbrand Hideout]] under [[Tresendar Manor]]
They were ordered to either run the party off, or try to capture them.  Kill them if neccessary.
