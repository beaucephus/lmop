====== Neverwinter ======
Created Wednesday 12 June 2019

Neverwinter is divided into districts.
	 The **City Core**, the central region of the city, where the ruling offices, and the main temples were located.[34]
	The **Peninsula District**, that comprised the southwestern quadrant of the city.[34]
	The **Docks District**, the main port.[34]
	The **Blacklake District**, located in the northeastern quadrant.[34]
	The **Beggar's Nest**, located in the southeast quadrant.[34]
	The **Arcanist Quarter**, located in the southeast quadrant.[13]
