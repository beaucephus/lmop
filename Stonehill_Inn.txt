====== Stonehill Inn ======
Created Sunday 13 January 2019

The Stonehill Inn is a newly build roadhouse of sorts and is the only inn in [[Phandalin]]
It's run by [[Toblen Stonehill]]
Toblen is a young human entrepreneur who is and old aquaintance of [[Sildar Hallwinter]]
