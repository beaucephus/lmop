====== Mailus Drapier's Summons ======
Created Tuesday 04 June 2019

[[:Mailus Drapier]] summons the party to his office.

He congratulates [[:Fie]] and [[:H'ruck]] for __cleaning up the Redbrand dogs__
He asks if the [[:Redbrands]] leader ( [[:Iarno Albrek aka Glasstaff]] ) is dead
  If not, he immediately gives the players a quest to find and kill him for __double the usually price__. (500gp) 

He expresses his pride in [[:Othard Drapier]] at helping __extend the family's influence.  You're finally starting to show promise like your brother!__

**Developments**
Mailus offers the players a quest in the city 
If Mailus learns that the party suspects him of being the mastermind behind the Redbrands, he determines they are too dangerous to be left alive and sends Othard's brother [[:Vincent Drapier]] and a hit squad to kill them all except Othard
