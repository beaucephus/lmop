====== Shrine of Luck ======
Created Tuesday 08 January 2019

Shrine to [[Tymora, Goddess of Luck]].

[[Sister Garaele]] lives in a small cottage behind the shrine and offers the party 3 Potions of Healing to retrieve an old wizard's spellbook from [[Agatha the Banshee]]
