====== Auntie Hilda ======
Created Wednesday 17 July 2019

**Green hag**

In disuise: __Hilda is an older womain with a walking cane and a warming smile.  The children play around her and call her "Auntie Hilda".__

Auntie Hilda runs an orphanage in the Beggar's Nest district of [[:Neverwinter]] called [[:Little Miracles Orphan Home]]
The **Annis hag** [[:Big Agnes]] corrupts the children until they are exiled or run away from their homes.
Then Auntie Hilda takes them into the orphanage.
There the children are prepared by the **night hag **[[:Black Ethel]] to be prepared and eventually consumed by one of the 3 hags.

Auntie Hilda is currently pregnant with child and runs the orphanage under disguise.
