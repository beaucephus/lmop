====== Urgala Meltimer ======
Created Saturday 10 August 2019

A retired adventurer, Urgala owns a respectable inn, the Northshield House, and she doesn't want to see it or her neighbors' homes destroyed. She has no tolerance for monsters or bullies.

**ideal** We live in a violent world, and sometimes violence is necessary for survival.
**bond** My home is my life. Threaten it, and I'll hurt you.
**flaw** I know how treacherous and greedy adventurers can be. I don't trust them.


===== Quest =====
In the aftermath after the battle, Urgala relates to the characters that one of her former adventuring companions, a wealthy knight named [[:Harthos Zymorven]] , had an [[:Item:Giant Slayer Great Axe]] .
The last time she spoke with him, he was living in [[:Zymorven Hall]] , the families keep on the [[:Rauvin Road]] northwest of [[:Silverymoon]]
Urgala thinks Harthos might be willing to part with the weapon with a suggestion from her.
She writes 2 letters to Harthos and asks the party to deliver them. One of them is a letter commendint the party and request Harthos help them.
