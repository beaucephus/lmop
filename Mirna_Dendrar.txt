====== Mirna Dendrar ======
Created Wednesday 19 June 2019

[[:Mirna Dendrar|Mirna Dendrar]] was abducted after the [[Redbrands]] killed her husband [[Thel Dendrar]], and was being prepared for sale into slavery.

The party rescued Mirna and she told them of her family's heirloom and alchemy shop in the [[:Ruins of Thundertree]]
