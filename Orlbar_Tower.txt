====== Orlbar Tower ======
Created Thursday 04 July 2019

This long abandoned wizard's tower was serving as a base for the [[:Zhentarim]] until recently when **stone giants** from [[:Deadstone Cleft]] attacked.

It is in disrepair when the party finds it beging dismantled by giants and costs 1,000gp and 10 days to repair.
