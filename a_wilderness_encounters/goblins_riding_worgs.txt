====== goblins riding worgs ======
Created Monday 07 January 2019

They come up behind the party and give chase.  They know the location of cragmaw castle.

loot: 5 gp per goblin, scimitar, and shortbow.  hide from worg if good roll.  Can be crafted into blanket/clothing with leatherworking proficiency.

goblins: challenge 1/4 (50 xp)
worgs: challenge 1/2 (100 xp)
