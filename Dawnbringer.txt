====== Dawnbringer ======
Created Sunday 13 January 2019

Lightbringer is a +1 magical mace that requires attunement by a good aligned character.
Once attuned, the wielder can speak the command word to make the mace glow like a torch.
The mace deals 1d6 extra radiant damage while glowing.
