====== Grung of Isla Nublar ======
Created Wednesday 02 October 2019

The Grun have a small (<50 members) village on Isla Nublar.

They subsist by enslaving dinosaurs and are at war the ever-encroaching Kuo-toans of the surrounding sea.

There are no red or yellow (higher castes) grung on the island.
Only green, blue, and purple (lower castes)

They are led by [[:Ja'Hen Zoog Slur'Mog Lurt Rahn]] , the only orange grung.
