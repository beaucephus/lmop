====== Dripping Caves ======
Created Friday 31 May 2019

A network of bat caves 1 mile north of [[Nightstone]]

A band of golins and 2 mated ogres have setup in the cave network.
After the giant attack on nightstone, the surviving villagers fled to the caves only to be captured by the goblins.
They are being kept as food...
