====== Dral Thelev ======
Created Tuesday 12 November 2019

The one-eyed half-orc proprietor of [[:Danivarr's House]] in [[:Everlund]]

''Dral's thin build and sharp attire contrasts his brutish, half-orc face, tusks, and missing eye.''
''A black monacle covers the scared divot where his eye once was.''

He is a member of the [[:Harpers]] and has a close bond to [[:Darathra Shendrel]] of [[:Triboar]]
