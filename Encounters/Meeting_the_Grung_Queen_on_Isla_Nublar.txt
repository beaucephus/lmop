====== Meeting the Grung Queen on Isla Nublar ======
Created Sunday 06 October 2019

Party finds grung queen [[:Ja'Hen Zoog Slur'Mog Lurt Rahn]] resting in a pond near the central island lake.

Her spinosaurus lies in waiting in the lake.

Ja'Hen asks H'ruk if these are his slaves.

**Developments**
Ja'hen might challenge Hruk to a duel.

She could take a liking to H'ruk, in which case she invites him as a guest to her village.
