====== Guards outside Cloak Tower ======
Created Tuesday 06 August 2019

The guards are standing around outside the cloak tower after the players exit.
They are shocked that her brother wasn't actually in the tower.
They personally know [[:Mary Sue]] the little girl.
They offer directions to her house if the players wish to tell her parents. __Her parents will want to hear about this!__

If the players go to her house, run [[:Encounters:Father Sue and Mary Sue at home]]
