====== Mating Triceratops ======
Created Wednesday 25 September 2019

The party hears sounds of a large animal in distress.

Upon approaching a clearing they see 2 triceratops mating.
Both attack the party if the party is noticed.


[[..\statblocks\triceratops.png]]

**Developments**
The triceratops male has viscious slashing scars across his rear and bits of torn harnessing around his chest and neck
