====== Grendel ======
Created Sunday 13 January 2019

Grendel is a chaotic evil nothic who currently inhabits the Crevasse in the [[Redbrand Hideout]] underneath [[Tresendar Manor]] in [[Phandalin]]
 The nothic was here before the redbrands, and made a deal with [[Iarno Albrek aka Glasstaff]] .  
  He guards the crevasse in exchange for treasure and food.  [[Grendel]] will betray Iarno for treasure or food.
  He speaks telepathicly and knows common.
  He offers deals to non-good players.
