====== Xolkin Alassandar ======
Created Friday 31 May 2019

Xolkin is a charming but ruthless male half-elf **bandit captain** in his mid thirties with a reddish/dark complexion and a long, thin scar stretching from his right cheekbone to his chin

He's in love with [[:Kella Darkhope]] even tho he knows she doesn't feel the same way about him.
He leads a gang of bandits called the "Seven Snakes".  Thy are the muscle for a [[:Zhentarim]] leader named [[:Snail]] based in [[Daggerford]]
