====== rickety tower ======
Created Wednesday 06 February 2019

**Only use in the early morning or late evening!!!**

30x30 ft base
60 ft height

Old watch tower or windmill.
The door has been boarded up from the inside, DC 22 strength check to break.
Describe the shadow depending on the time of day.

Kobolds have buried underneath and occupy the top.
They've carved wooden slits into the tower.
They take turns at the slit to shoot shortbows, taking half cover.  Otherwise they are in full cover.
Must use reaction to hit at half cover.

The tower should be nearby a dragon probably.
