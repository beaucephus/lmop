====== Amulet of Health(FoS) ======
Created Wednesday 17 July 2019

Adventuring Gear (Neck)
Rare

Requires Attunement

While you wear this swirling, sparkling ruby amulet, your Constitution score is increased by 6 (to a maximum of 22).
