====== Amulet of Health(FoS) ======
Created Wednesday 17 July 2019

Adventuring Gear (Neck)
Rare

Requires Attunement

While you wear this swirling, sparkling ruby amulet, your Constitution score becomes 19.
Any time you recover hit points, gain 5 temporary hit points.
