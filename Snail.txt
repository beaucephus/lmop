====== Snail ======
Created Thursday 04 July 2019

The Snail aka [[:Nelkin Danniker]] is a plump weasel of a halfling.

He's known to his associates as the Snail because of his slow, deliberate way of talking.
He relaxes at the [[:River Shining Tavern and Inn]] in [[:Daggerford]] , watched over by 6 **thugs** posing as patrons.
