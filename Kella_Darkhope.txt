====== Kella Darkhope ======
Created Thursday 30 May 2019

Kella is a blonde human female with sharp facial features underneath her plain brown travelling cloak.

Kella is a [[Zhentarim]] **spy** sent to [[Nightstone]] in the guise of a travelling monk.

**Motivation**
Kella is waiting in Nightstone for her Zent allies to arrive. She intends to take over the village and turn it into a [[Black Network]] base.
