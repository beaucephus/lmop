====== Warm Wood ======
Created Monday 05 August 2019

A dense, temperate rain forest running along the Sword Coast just south of [[:Neverwinter]] 

It has natural terrain consisting of towering cliffs and caves formed from ancient underwater coral and is full of coral and sea shells
