====== Reidoth's Unicorn ======
Created Wednesday 19 June 2019

A hand-carved statuette of a unicorn with [[:Reidoth the Druid]] 's initials on it.
If presented, it grants advantage on any checks to interact with a member of the [[:Emerald Enclave]]
