====== Droop ======
Created Wednesday 09 January 2019

Droop is a cowardly goblin being bullied by bugbears in the [[Redbrand Hideout:9.Guard Barracks]] of the [[Redbrand Hideout]].

Droop joins the party for a while if asked, but flees as soon as they get outdoors.
