====== Iarno Albrek aka Glasstaff ======
Created Sunday 13 January 2019

Iarno Albrek is a former member of the [[Lord's Alliance]]
He was sent to [[Phandalin]] to set up a constabulary, but instead create the bandit gang calling them, the [[Redbrands]]

HP 50+
AC 16(mage armor + Staff of Defense)
+7 to hit
15 spell save DC

Cantrips (at will): mage hand, minor illusion, poison spray, ray of frost
1st level (4 slots): mage armor, magic missile, shield, charm person
2nd level (3 slots): alter self, misty step, web
3rd level (2 slots): fly, lightning bolt

Staff of Defense
+1 AC
10 charges
  Mage Armor (1 charge)
  Shield (2 charges)
