====== Atan ======
Created Sunday 17 February 2019

Atan is a male Yuan-ti pureblood darkness sorcerer
He is seeking the [[Forge of Spells]] and knows the secrets of how to operate it.

**Alignment**
Lawful Evil

**Backstory**
Atan was a human until he was kidnapped by by [[Ras Nsi]] and converted into a yuan-ti pureblood by a heinous ritual.
Atan was sent by Ras Nsi on a mission to find and take control of the [[Forge of Spells]] for the yuan-ti.
