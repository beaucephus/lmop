====== Halia Thornton ======
Created Sunday 13 January 2019

Halia Thornton is a sharp witted, beautiful, and ambitious young human woman who runs the [[Phandalin Miner's Exchange]] in [[Phandalin]]
She is a member of the secretive organization called the [[Zhentarim]]
She offers the players a quest to eliminate "Glasstaff", the leader of the [[Redbrands]] and bring her any correspondence in his possession. (100gp)
She secretly wants to take over the [[Redbrands]] herself.
