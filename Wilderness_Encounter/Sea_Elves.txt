====== Sea Elves ======
Created Saturday 31 August 2019

A half-dozen sea elves on wooden out-rigger sailing canoes speed up to the party.

They offer to guide the party to their destination.

They know where [[:Maelstrom]] is and can guide the party there.
They warn the party about the whirlpool leading to it.
