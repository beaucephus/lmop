====== Uthgardt Barbarians ======
Created Saturday 31 August 2019

14 **tribal warriors**
1 **uthgardt shaman** (appendix C)

Pick the appropriate tribe based on where the party encounters them.
The barbarians carry no treasure, but they have left an easy-to-follow trail as they travelled.

The travelling barbarians are outfitted with bows, spears, and large sacks.

The sacks contain various quantities of wild game, mushrooms, and herbs.

**development**
If the party follows the barbarians or backtracks their trail, it leads to the closest [[:Uthgardt Ancestral Spirit Mounds]]
