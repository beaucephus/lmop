====== Blackstaff's hit squad ======
Created Tuesday 13 August 2019

The party destroyed [[:Blackstaff]] 's clone in [[:Rassalantar]] and he is angry.
Blackstaff sends a hit squad of rogues, fighters and bards after the party.
Have them track the party and ambush when advantageous. __Blackstaff sends his most generous regards.__

+4 to damage
