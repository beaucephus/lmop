====== Driftwood Tavern ======
Created Wednesday 26 June 2019

The Driftwood Tavern is a fine inn and tavern, which also doubles as a museum
Drinks, meals, and rooms are quite expensive in the Driftwood Tavern, catering to a richer clientele than other similar establishments in Neverwinter.

Owned and run by [[:Azura Brey]]

**History**
The Driftwood Tavern was built years before 1385 DR, and it survived both the Spellplague and the eruption of Mount Hotenow nearly unscathed.
