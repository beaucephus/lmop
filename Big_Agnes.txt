====== Big Agnes ======
Created Wednesday 17 July 2019

**Annis hag** in the [[:Beggar's Nest Coven]] in [[:Neverwinter]]

She corrupts young children in town by giving them pendants of her teeth.
Through her psychic link, she corrupts the children until they either run away or are cast away.
Once the children are forsaken [[:Auntie Hilda]] accepts them into her orphanage 


Agnes lives in the woods near [[:Neverwinter]]

run [[:Dungeon:Auntie Agnes' Lair]]
