====== Theodore Anthony Nugent ======
Created Saturday 12 January 2019

aka Ted, Uncle Ted, The Nuge

Ted is the physician/undertaker in [[Phandalin]].
He lives and works out of his small clinic just east of the [[Sleeping Giant]].
The undertaker's workshop is out back, separate from his home/clinic.

Ted needs large quantities of spider venom for medicine (good for arthritis).
He knows that abnormaly large spiders can be found in the [[Neverwinter Wood]] and [[Ruins of Thundertree]].
