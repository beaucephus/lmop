====== Ruins of Conyberry ======
Created Friday 25 January 2019

Long ruined town of a few dozen buildings.  It was overrun during the orc invasions 500 years ago.

A large group of Ankhegs burrow in the southern end.

The players defeated the [[1 wilderness encounters:ankheg]] here.
