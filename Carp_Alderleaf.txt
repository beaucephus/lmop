====== Carp Alderleaf ======
Created Tuesday 08 January 2019

Carp Alderleaf is the halfling son of Qelline Alderleaf.  He is thouroughly enchanted by the idea of becoming an adventerur.  He lives and plays around the [[Alderleaf Farm]] in [[Phandalin]].

Carp found a cave with some [[Redbrands]] leaving.  It leads into the [[Redbrand Hideout]].
