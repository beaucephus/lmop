====== Carp Alderleaf ======
Created Tuesday 08 January 2019

Carp Alderleaf is the halfling son of Qelline Alderleaf.  He lives and plays around the [[Alderleaf Farm]] in [[Phandalin]].

Carp knows a secret way into the [[Redbrand Hideout]].
