====== Giants ======
Created Sunday 06 October 2019

===== Cloud Giants =====
**Wind Aura.** A magical aura of wind surrounds the giant.
10-foot radius sphere that the giant must concentrate on.
+2 to AC. +2 to DEX saves
Enemies must make a DC20 STR save or be pushed to the edge of the sphere and be knocked prone.

===== Fire Giants =====
**Siege Monster.** Fire giants do double damage to objects and structures.

===== Frost Giants =====
**Weighted Net.** Ranged Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, range 20/60ft., one large or smaller creature.
Hit: The target is restrained until it escapes the net.
DC17 STR check to escape.
The net has AC 12 and 15HP, Immune to piercing and bludgeoning damage.

===== Stone Giants =====
**Rolling Rock.** The giant sends a rock tubling along the ground in a 30-foot line that is 5-foot wide.
DC17 DEX save or take 4d10 bludgeoning damage and be knocked prone.

===== Storm Giants =====
**Thunderous Stomp (Recharge 6).** The storm giant stomps the ground, triggering a thunderclap.
All creature within 15 feet make DC17 CON save or take 6d10 thunder damage and be deafened for 10 minutes.
Half damage and no deafening on a successful save.
The thunderclap can be heard for half a mile.

===== All giants have the below abilities in addition to their normal statblocks. =====

**Kick.** Replaces 1 attack. The giant tries to punt a small or medium creature within 10 feet of it.
DC20 Dex save or be hurled 1d6 x 10 feet horizontally and land prone.
1d8 bludgeoning damage for every 10 feet thrown.

**Restrain and Bite.** Whenever a giant grapples, the target is restrained in addition to being grappled.
On the next turn after grappling an enemy, the giant can attempt to bite the target. Bite deals half the giant's hit dice in damage and recieves a lingering injury.
