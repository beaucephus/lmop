====== Staff of the Red Oak ======
Created Saturday 12 January 2019

Staff, rare (require attunement by a druid)

This staff can be weilded as a magic quarterstaff that grants +1 to attack and damage rolls made with it.

You can use an action to speak the command word and plant one end of the staff in fertile earth to transform the the staff into a healty tree.
The tree is 60 feet tall and has a 5-foot diameter trunk.  
While touching the tree, you can speak a second command word to revert the tree to a staff.
