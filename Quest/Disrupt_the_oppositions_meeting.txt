====== Disrupt the oppositions meeting ======
Created Tuesday 06 August 2019

Lord Ulthein or some other Lord is an outspoken activist gaining popularity among the people of [[:Neverwinter]]

He's already staged several rally's that have almost ended in riots. He is dangerous and represents a threat to the safety and security of Neverwinter.

Mailus has intelligence that he will be meeting an emmissary from another nation to discuss the forceful overthrowing of Mailus' regime should Mailus dissolve the Lord's Council.


The party is tasked to lead a contingent of 20 men to disrupt the meeting.
	Unbeknowst to them, the men have orders to kill all the participants.
