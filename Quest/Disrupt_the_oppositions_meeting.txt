====== Disrupt the oppositions meeting ======
Created Tuesday 06 August 2019

Lord Ulthein is an outspoken activist gaining popularity among the people of [[:Neverwinter]]

He's already staged several rallies that have almost ended in riots. He is dangerous and represents a threat to the safety and security of Neverwinter.

Mailus has intelligence that he will be meeting an emmissary from another nation to discuss the forceful overthrowing of Mailus' regime should Mailus dissolve the Lord's Council.


The party is tasked to stop the meeting and leave no-one alive.
Unbeknowst to the party, [[:Slarkrethel]] has sent an aboleth, [[:K'oth the Slither]] , and the local Kuo-toans to make sure all the giants die.
