===== Hag Coven =====

**annis hag **has given an **Iron Token** to a local orphan.  The orphan does bad things. Do a bad thing to the party.

**night hag** uses her **Nightmare Haunting** ability on a party member if the annis hag is killed.

**green hag** has 2 young human girls as servants (they're under-aged hags).  She's eyeing another couple and wants to eat their baby.
