====== Assasinate the High Priest of Tyr ======
Created Tuesday 06 August 2019

The High Priest of Tyr [[:Anin Halley]] is an outspoken activist gaining popularity among the people of [[:Neverwinter]]

He's already staged several rally's that have almost ended in riots. He is dangerous and represents a threat to the safety and security of Neverwinter.

He must die or disappear and it must look like an accident.
