====== Gundren Rockseeker ======
Created Thursday 10 January 2019

Gundren Rockseeker is the eldest of the Rockseeker brothers.
He and his brothers have discovered the entrance to [[Wave echo cave]], the location of the [[Lost Mine of Phandelver]].
Gundren is currently being held captive at [[Cragmaw castle]].

[[Jober]] has a sharp distaste for Gundren.
