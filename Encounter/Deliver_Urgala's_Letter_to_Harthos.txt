====== Deliver Urgala's Letter to Harthos ======
Created Monday 02 September 2019

takes place in [[:Zymorven Hall]]

Lord Zymorven is sad to report that his son Harthal has stolen the [[:Item:Giant Slayer Great Axe]]
Harthal stole the axe and ran away after Lord Zymorven disowned him for marrying a common thief from [[:Yartar]]

If the characters go to [[:Yartar]]
The thieves guild [[:Hand of Yartar]] knows where Harthal is and the whereabouts of the sword.
  300gp and 6 hours for the thieve's guild to find the info.
  
Harthal was arrested and imprisoned for murdering a man.
A corrupt watch captain named Tholzar Brenner wields the axe now.

Brenner is a **knight** and travels with 8 **guards**

Any faction that a player has a recommendation from or is a member of can help them out. DC15
