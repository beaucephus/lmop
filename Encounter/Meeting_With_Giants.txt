====== Meeting With Giants ======
Created Wednesday 11 September 2019

Lords of [[:Neverwinter]] and an envoy of storm giants from [[:Maelstrom]] including [[:Queen Neri]] plan to meet on a small island off the sword coast.

They plan to discuss tactics and roles in the [[:Coup of Neverwinter]]
