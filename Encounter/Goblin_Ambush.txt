====== Goblin Ambush ======
Created Saturday 25 May 2019
@encounter

4 [[:Forked Ear Goblins]] ambush the party on the way to [[::Nightstone|Nightstone]]
2 use shortbows from 40ft in the forest, full cover except when they shoot,
2 rush into melee with scimitars.
If the melee goblins are killed, the archers flee.

AC 15
HP 7
+6 to hit
+2 damage
shortbow
scimitar
